High Demand Areas

Msabaha and Mtondia areas continue to show the strongest demand due to excellent road infrastructure and proximity to major highways. Properties in these locations sell 40% faster than other areas.

Avg. Sale Time: 30 days Demand: Very High

Infrastructure Development

The completion of Bofa Road (B69) tarmacking has significantly increased property values in the area. Properties near tarmacked roads show 50% higher appreciation rates compared to murram roads.

Value Increase: +50% Infrastructure: Excellent

Properties near Malindi International Airport command premium prices due to high tourism potential and commercial development opportunities. Rental yields in this area average 12-15% annually.

ROI: 12-15% Commercial Potential: High
