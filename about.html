About Kilifi Properties

Building Dreams, Securing Futures

Who We Are

Your Trusted Land Investment Partner

Kilifi Properties is a leading real estate agency in the coastal region of Kenya, dedicated to providing reliable and affordable land investment opportunities. We specialize in selling genuine plots of land in Nyali, Mtwapa, Kikambala, Kilifi, Bofa, Watamu, Tezo, Chumani, and Malindi.

With a strong reputation built on trust, transparency, and professionalism, we help clients acquire titled plots suitable for both residential and commercial development. Our team is committed to offering expert guidance, from site visits to title processing, ensuring every purchase is secure and stress-free.

At Kilifi Properties, we understand the value of land as a long-term investment. That's why we carefully select prime locations that promise growth, accessibility, and appreciation. Whether you're a first-time buyer or a seasoned investor, we are your reliable partner in coastal real estate.
